Adding calendar to Outlook

Copy this address:

https://www.google.com/calendar/ical/olineventcalendar%40gmail.com/public/basic.ics

Open Outlook and go to the top menu, clicking Tools → Account Settings. In Account Settings choose the Internet Calendars tab, click on New... and Paste the URL you just copied from Google into the location and click Add.

You then get to the subscriptions screen. Give the calendar a name and description and click OK.

You can now see the calendar in the list in the Outlook sidebar panel.

Source: http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/microsoft-office/view-your-google-calendar-in-outlook-2007/

Adding an event

There are two ways to simply add an event to this calendar:
  • Events with iCals sent to the carpediem@lists.olin.edu mailing list are automatically added to this calendar.
  • To add an event directly, attach an iCal to an email sent to olineventcalendar@gmail.com. NOTE: This email must come from an @students.olin.edu address.
